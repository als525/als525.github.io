Features


Synaptic pruning occurs with every habit you build. Your brain builds a strong network of neurons to support your current behaviors. The more you do something, the stronger and more efficient the connection becomes. You probably have very strong habits and connections that you take for granted each day. For example, your brain is probably very efficient at remembering to take a shower each morning or to brew your morning cup of coffee or to open the blinds when the sun rises … or thousands of other daily habits. You can take advantage of these strong connections to build new habits like gratitude journaling. So, everytime you engage in any self-care activity, why not also include 5 minutes with your Gratitude Positive Power app


Treat your mental health like you would your physical health. Gratitude journalling has many proven benefits from improving self-esteem, physical health, your relationship, improves empathy and reduces agression and you will be guaranteed to sleep better. The Gratitude Positive Power App is a place where you can keep track of your progress and review it over time.


The Gratitude Journal app offers a structure framework to organise your gratitude thoughts and feelings from the day. It provides 5 different sections you can complete every morning and every night. The app is an easy and friendly format to help you complete your gratitude journaling on the go and will help you replace your tradiational journal.
